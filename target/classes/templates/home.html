Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What is the Railway Management System?

The Railway Management System is a platform that allows users to book train tickets, find routes, and manage their travel plans efficiently.

How can I book a ticket?

Click on the "Book Ticket" button on the home page, choose your route, fill in the details, and confirm your booking.
